Machine Learning Engineer

I am a Researcher and Engineer in Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval, and Generative AI, focused on building production ready LLM systems. My background is in Software Engineering and applied machine learning, with an emphasis on scalable retrieval, evaluation, and deployment for real world applications.

My work focuses on two main directions. The first is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and hybrid search, where I design and optimize end-to-end retrieval pipelines to make LLM outputs more grounded, accurate, and useful. The second is agentic GenAI systems and LLMOps, where I build tool using agents and the supporting infrastructure required for production: LLM deployment and configuration, CI/CD, monitoring/observability, reliability evaluation, and cost/latency optimization, often across Python/TypeScript and cloud-native environments.
